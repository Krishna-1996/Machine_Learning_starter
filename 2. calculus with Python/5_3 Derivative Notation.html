Derivative Notations

We have different notation to notify the Derivative, some are old and some are new as per the use.

  1. First Derivative (Prime Notation):

    2. Unicode: f'(x)
    HTML: f'(x)

  2. Second Derivative (Double Prime Notation):

    3. Unicode: f''(x)
    HTML: f''(x)

  3. Partial Derivative: (The one I use here)

    4. Unicode: ∂f/∂x
    HTML: ∂f/∂x

  4. Gradient (Nabla Operator):

    5. Unicode: ∇f
    HTML: ∇f

  5. Differential Operator (Leibniz Notation):

    6. Unicode: dy/dx
    HTML: dy/dx

  6. Laplacian Operator:

    7. Unicode: ∇²f
    HTML: ∇²f

  7. Directional Derivative:

    8. Unicode: ∂f/∂v
    HTML: ∂f/∂v

  8. Total Derivative:

    9. Unicode: df/dx
    HTML: df/dx
